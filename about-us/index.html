ODERINT DUM METUANT

"I believe that understanding history is not just about memorizing dates or names. For me, history is about learning how people think, why decisions are made, and how small choices can shape entire civilizations. As a CEO and Founder of a company dedicated to RTS game guides and personal coaching, I see history as one of the most powerful tools we have to prepare younger generations for strategic thinking in the real world.

RTS games naturally mirror historical dynamics: resource management, diplomacy, technological progression, warfare, and leadership under pressure. When kids play these games, they are not just having fun. They are making decisions similar to those faced by historical leaders, even if they do not realize it yet. This creates a unique opportunity. By connecting in-game mechanics to real historical events, we can spark curiosity and make history feel alive rather than distant or boring.

At our company, we use RTS guides and one-on-one assistance to turn gameplay into a learning experience. When a child learns why controlling trade routes matters in a game, we can link that to historical empires that rose and fell for the same reasons. When they struggle with managing multiple fronts, we can introduce lessons from real-world conflicts and leadership challenges. This approach transforms history from a subject they have to study into a story they want to explore.

I strongly believe that RTS games can act as a gateway to deeper historical knowledge. By meeting kids where their interests already are and guiding them with the right context, we can inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and a genuine appreciation for history. That is not just good education, it is an investment in smarter, more thoughtful future leaders."
